Our productive project

The idea was born from a personal experience where I worked as a fabric seller and I always imagined making pajamas to have my own business, another experience I have had with pajamas is that some garments are itchy, very hot, and the seams unravel.

Our pajamas company "francyluna" is dedicated to the manufacture of nightwear for the entire Colombian family, we will focus 50% on the female line, since it is in greater demand. And through women the different markets move since they always think of the whole family.

It is we are located in the city of Bogotá. The main objective is to offer our clients unforgettable experiences, a feeling of comfort, freshness, for their restful nights. We want to be recognized in the market for our quality, price, and good service.

Maintain the loyalty of our customers, through good customer service and after-sales monitoring. In the long term we want to be recognized nationally and internationally, to be able to position our brand, initially you will use E-Commerce for the distribution, sale, of our products, we are aware that the market is very competitive and we do not know electronic commerce very well.

We handle different types of fabrics such as polycottons, thermal polyester, poly lycra, 100% cotton for different types of cold, warm, and temperate climates. We have three segments female, male, child, and different models of pajamas such as long pants, shorts, robe, assortment of beautiful colors.
